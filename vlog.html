I've always loved traveling—exploring unseen nature & cultures—thanks to my dad, who dragged us around the world growing up. My mom was the one filming it all, turning those trips into cherished memories, and that's why I started editing with PowerDirector since middle school (now I'm on Premiere Pro, might switch to DaVinci Resolve in the future)

Now, I make vlogs for big & small adventures with family & friends—nothing fancy, maybe boring to outsiders, but it always brings smiles to myself & the crew.
